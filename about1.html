About Us

At MAF-DELECANS, we believe that mathematics is not just a subject but a way of thinking and exploring the world. Our club's name is an acronym that stands for the key areas we focus on: Matrices, Algebra, Fractions, Differential Equations, Linear Equations, Calculus and Analysis, and the Numeral System.

Whether you're fascinated by the elegance of algebraic structures, the intricacies of calculus, or the practical applications of differential equations, MAF-DELECANS has something for everyone. We offer a variety of activities, including interactive workshops, sessions, guest lectures, and club activities. These experiences are designed to enhance your mathematical skills, encourage critical thinking, and inspire a lifelong love for the subject.

MAF-DELECANS is open to all students, regardless of their background or level of expertise. Join us as we explore the fascinating world of mathematics, tackle challenging problems, and discover new perspectives.
