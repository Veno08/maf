About Manthanein

What is Manthanein?

Manthanein is the annual fest organized by the MAF-DELECANS club, celebrated as a day of mathematical exploration and fun. This one-day event is packed with a variety of engaging activities, including mathematical and non-mathematical events.

This fest is an unique opportunity for participants to demonstrate their skills, engage with like-minded individuals, and explore new mathematical concepts. Manthanein is not just about competition; it's a platform for learning, collaboration, and discovery.

Whether you're looking to dive into complex problems, or enjoy a casual math game, Manthanein has something for everyone. Come and be a part of Manthanein, where numbers come alive, equations tell stories, and every challenge is a chance to grow. It's a day dedicated to celebrating the joy of mathematics!
